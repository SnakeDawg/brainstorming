## Vision

Customers should be able to answer their own questions and complete their own
account tasks without contacting support. The portal is the product's front
door — every interaction should feel fast, obvious, and trustworthy.

## Objectives for 2026

| # | Objective | Measure |
|---|---|---|
| O1 | Reduce support ticket volume | −30% tickets per active customer by Q4 |
| O2 | Increase self-service adoption | 60% of account changes done in-portal |
| O3 | Improve perceived performance | p75 page load under 2s on key flows |

## Strategic pillars

Roadmap items and initiative proposals must trace back to one of these:

1. **Self-service first** — every common support request gets an in-portal flow.
2. **Speed as a feature** — front-end performance is a product requirement, not a cleanup task.
3. **Trust and clarity** — plain language, visible system status, no dead ends.
