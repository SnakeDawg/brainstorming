# Round 3 — Gap Surfacing

Step out of free-form discussion. Hermes (the moderator) drives this round
explicitly — you are now going to make the team enumerate **what they don't know
and who owns what**.

Produce four sections, in order:

## 1. Missing inputs

A table with columns: **Persona | Input they need | Why they can't move without it
| Likely owner**. Aim for at least 8 rows total. Pull from the "missing input"
each persona named in round 1, plus anything new that surfaced in round 2.

## 2. Unresolved conflicts

A bulleted list of every real tradeoff still on the table from round 2 — name the
two (or more) personas, the position each holds, and the **decision that has not
yet been made**. Do not propose resolutions yet. Aim for at least 5 items.

## 3. Unclear ownership

A bulleted list of decisions or deliverables where it is not obvious who is
accountable. Format each as:
- **<decision / deliverable>** — currently unclear: <list of personas who think
  it might be theirs or someone else's>

Aim for at least 3 items.

## 4. Hidden assumptions

A bulleted list of assumptions any persona is making that have not been validated
in this conversation (e.g., "Sales is assuming pricing flexibility that PDM
hasn't confirmed", "Marketing is assuming the launch date will hold"). Aim for at
least 4 items.
