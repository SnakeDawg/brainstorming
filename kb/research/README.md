## Adding research

- Copy [`_template.md`](_template.md), or
- Open a **Research task** issue from the
  [issue forms](https://github.com/SnakeDawg/brainstorming/issues/new/choose).
  Record findings in the issue as you go; when the issue is closed, the
  automation files the write-up here.
